I'm Mostafa — a Senior Product Designer working at the intersection of business strategy, user research, and engineering reality. I don't just design interfaces; I design systems that scale.

Over 6+ years, I've led design across fintech, SaaS, and mobile — embedded within product and engineering teams at Kuwait Finance House Egypt, Basata, Bounce, and EButler. Real products, real constraints, real users.

My process is rigorous: research-led, systems-oriented, and anchored in measurable outcomes. I collaborate closely with PMs and engineers — speaking their language, understanding their trade-offs, and designing solutions that ship on time and hold up at scale.

I believe clarity is the hardest thing to design. Everything else is decoration.
